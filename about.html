AIKMCC Hyderabad Beyond Charity, A Thriving Malayali Hub

Hyderabad is home to a vibrant Malayali community, and AIKMCC Hyderabad stands as a beacon of unity and support. More than just a charitable organization, AIKMCC fosters social, cultural, and sporting activities, strengthening bonds among Malayalis.

From providing medical aid to supporting stranded expatriates, AIKMCC has led numerous impactful initiatives since its inception in 2018. With annual gatherings, sports events, and unwavering community service, it continues to be a pillar of strength for Malayalis in Hyderabad.

Our Vision

AIKMCC is a thriving Malayali community outside Kerala that fosters artistic, social, cultural, and charitable initiatives. As an integral part of KMCC, the world's largest expatriate organization, AIKMCC operates across various Indian cities to support Malayalis. Beyond humanitarian and charity-driven efforts, the organization plays a key role in social and cultural spheres, earning a cherished place in the hearts of Malayalis through its unwavering commitment to service.

Our Mission

AIKMCC stands for service, humanity, medical aid, compassion, love, and commitment. The organization strives to wipe away the tears of the less fortunate, understand their struggles, stand by them in times of need, protect them in adversity, and bring peace to distressed hearts.

Building a just and progressive nation requires an educated and culturally enriched society. With a focus on brotherhood, love, and healthcare, AIKMCC is committed to long-term social and cultural initiatives that contribute to national development and social reform.
